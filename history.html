Our Story

Odisha Lalit Kala Akademi was created as the State‚Äôs apex body for visual arts with the objective of nurturing painting, sculpture, graphics and allied arts across Odisha. Conceived under the Department of Tourism & Culture (Culture), the Akademi emerged as a platform where traditional Odia artistic heritage and contemporary creative practices could grow together.

From its early years, the Akademi has worked to connect artists, institutions and audiences, organizing exhibitions, camps, workshops and awards programmes that recognize and support creative excellence throughout the State.
