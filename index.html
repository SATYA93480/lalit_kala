art & culture MAGAZINE

ART & CULTURE has been published continuously by the Odisha Lalit Kala Academy, which is dedicated to supporting artistic inquiry and to fostering the pursuit of knowledge about Odisha's rich cultural heritage. The January/February 2026 issue features the Top 10 Art Exhibitions of 2025. Other stories include the origins of Pattachitra art, traditional sculptures of Konark, and the revival of classical Odissi dance forms.

EXPLORE THE MAGAZINE SEARCH THE ARCHIVE

ODISHA JOURNAL of art & culture

The Odisha Journal has set the standard for respected scholarship of Odia art and culture since 1950. The January 2026 issue contains articles on temple architecture of Bhubaneswar, traditional handicrafts of tribal Odisha, classical music traditions, and contemporary art movements. Open-access content includes a comprehensive study of Pattachitra painting techniques, a review of the Tribal Art: Heritage of Odisha exhibition, and letters from renowned artists and scholars.
